Vodka Recipes

Penne alla vodka

Ingredients

  • penne, pennette, mezze maniche, pipe, whatever you want;
  • 1 little onion;
  • pancetta;
  • cream;
  • 2 big shots of vodka (one for the pasta, one for you);
  • tomato sauce;
  • olive oil;
  • salt

Procedure

While the water boils, cut the little onion in tiny tiny pieces and put them in a pan with a bit of olive oil. Wait for them to become clear like tasty little jewels. Don't burn them! Then you put the pancetta and open the window if you don't want that all of your rooms smell like pancetta (not that it'd be a bad thing though). Now you put the shot of vodka and hear the majestic sound, the hiss of pleasure! Drink the other shot while you compliment yourself and the pasta. Cheers! Now put the tomato sauce, stir, put some salt if you want (or sugar! mmmmh), stir, put the cream, stir... Voil√†! OH NO, you forgot the boiling water! Quickly, put the salt, put the pasta! Once it's ready drop the pasta in the pan and stir everything and enjoy your simple yet edgy dish and the rest of your lazy day.
